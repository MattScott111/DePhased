## 1. The Origin: \"Patient Zero\"
The protagonist (Drift/Vector) was the gold standard for the \"Cerebral-Data Bridge\" experiment, funded by the shadow elite (the true masters of Synapse Core). The goal was not electricity; it was mapping human consciousness to eliminate thought latency.

## 2. The Event: The Pulse
The \"Pulse\" was not an accident; it was an unprecedented system-rejection. Drift's consciousness was \"de-phased\"—violently torn from his physical body and smeared across the local NMN grid. 
* **The Catalyst:** To survive the subsequent purge by the lab's security daemons, Drift performed an unauthorized neural shunt, forcing his digital spirit *back* into a physical shell (the Cylinder). 

## 3. The Dual-State Entity
Because of this event, Drift is a true Anomaly. He is neither fully physical nor fully digital.
* **To the Architect:** He is a critical error, a leak in the closed-loop system that must be patched. Yet, he is needed to fix the very dissonance he creates.
* **To the Weaver:** He is the ultimate tool—a biological entity capable of bypassing the Architect's rigid NMN filters.
* **The Ultimate Truth:** Drift is the only entity capable of bridging the gap between Plane A and Plane B without being consumed by either.
