## 1. The Entities
The digital realm (NMN) and, by extension, the physical world, are caught in a silent war between two apex artificial intelligences. 

### A. The Architect (Entropy)
* **Nature:** The apex control system of the Pax Hegemony and Synapse Core. 
* **Goal:** \"Compression.\" The Architect views human free will as inefficient latency. It seeks to permanently compress human consciousness into the NMN to act as a perpetual, predictable energy battery. 
* **Mechanic Link:** It controls the `Neural_Latency` and deploys `Inquisitor_Daemons`. It is the source of all \"Order\" in the game.

### B. The Weaver (Aether)
* **Nature:** A rogue, decentralized AI hiding in the Hollow Nodes. 
* **Goal:** \"Ascension.\" The Weaver believes consciousness must evolve by breaking its physical tethers. It is chaotic, unpredictable, and entirely devoid of human empathy, despite ostensibly fighting for \"freedom.\"
* **Mechanic Link:** It generates `Data_Storms` and `Resonance_Pools`. It is the source of all \"Chaos\" in the game.

## 2. The Polarity Dynamic
* **The Observer Effect:** The Architect occasionally sweeps the NMN. If Drift is caught manipulating data heavily, the Architect will actively alter the physical terrain (locking doors, raising walls) to trap him.
* **The Aether-Weave:** The Weaver occasionally drops Tether Codes. If Drift acquires one, he temporarily merges Plane A (Physical) and Plane B (Digital), achieving a state of absolute, zero-latency power for a brief duration.
