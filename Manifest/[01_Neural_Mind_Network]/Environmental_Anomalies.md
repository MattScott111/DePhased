## 1. System Overview
The NMN is not a static plane; it has its own dynamic "weather" and ecosystem. These anomalies affect both the digital navigation (Plane B) and physical interactions (Plane A).

## 2. The Digital Weather
* **Data Storms (Static Squalls):** Massive, roaming clouds of corrupted data. 
    * *Mechanic:* If Drift is De-Phased inside a storm, `Neural_Latency` spikes. Physical collision bounds may temporarily fail, causing him to drop through physical floors or get trapped.
* **Resonance Pools:** Pockets of perfectly harmonized NMN left by the Weaver AI.
    * *Mechanic:* Standing in these zones while De-Phased grants passive healing to the physical cylinder by optimizing its molecular data array.

## 3. The Feral Data (Leviathans)
In deep, unmapped sectors, there exist massive, feral aggregations of lost human consciousness. They roam the NMN like digital whales.
* *Mechanic:* Drift cannot fight them. He must survive them or "hitch a ride" by matching their frequency, allowing for massive fast-travel leaps across the NMN grid.
