## 1. The Substrate (The World Between Worlds)
The Neural Mind Network (NMN) is the fundamental data-layer connecting all consciousness. It is not an alternate dimension; it is the raw code executing the physical reality.

## 2. The Dual-Plane Physics Model
The engine tracks two simultaneous states for the player and environment:
* **Plane A (Physical Reality):** Governed by the `Kinetic_Actor_Engine`. Solid walls, gravity, NPCs, and physical guards.
* **Plane B (Digital Matrix):** The NMN layer. Accessible via `Phased_Mode`. Walls become semi-permeable terrain, and data-streams flow like rivers. 

## 3. Core Mechanics of the NMN
* **Resonance Signatures:** Every entity (NPC, Daemon, Door) emits a frequency. Drift must attune to these frequencies to interact with them digitally.
* **Neural Latency (The Tax):** Moving through sectors controlled by major factions (e.g., Pax Hegemony) incurs a \"Latency Tax.\" The higher the faction's control, the more sluggish Drift's NMN abilities become due to massive data-filtration.
* **Dissonance Tracking:** The `NMN_State_Observer` constantly tracks the delta between Plane A and Plane B. If Drift causes too much chaos in Plane B (high dissonance), digital security daemons are spawned to hunt him.
