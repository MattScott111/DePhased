## 1. System Overview
Because Drift is an anomaly, combat and traversal are treated as data manipulation rather than traditional physical strikes.

## 2. Combat: Somatic Echoing (Ghost-Striking)
* **Concept:** Drift cannot punch a digital daemon, but he can corrupt a physical enemy's digital anchor.
* **Mechanic:** Drift enters `Phased_Mode` (Plane B), grabs an enemy's digital signature, and "drags" it out of alignment. Upon returning to Plane A, the physical enemy takes massive damage as its body violently attempts to catch up to its displaced digital signature.

## 3. Traversal: Architectural Subversion
* **Concept:** Hacking physical barriers via digital frequency bridging.
* **Mechanic:** Instead of finding a physical key, Drift De-Phases to find a locked door's digital "Node." By forcing the node's frequency to align with a Rebel Clan's frequency, the door opens for Drift but actively attacks Corporate Guards who attempt to pass, as its friend/foe identification has been inverted.
