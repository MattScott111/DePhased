# Corporate Dossier: CEO Victoria Vance (The Voltaic Syndicate)

## 🔍 System Purpose
Defines the economic corporate parameters, asset tracking vectors, and underlying motivation matrices for the secondary corporate antagonist.

## 🌀 Lore & Aesthetic Integration
The pristine, ruthless, high-society tech-baroness who sits at the absolute peak of the Voltaic Syndicate. Clad in a tailored dress woven from smart-liquid conduction fibers, she commands the global Deuterium cells with a wave of her hand. She does not share Sterling's fanatical desire to control the populace—she simply wants absolute financial supremacy over the market token values.
