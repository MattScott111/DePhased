# Character Dossier: Silas "The Signal" Vance

## 🔍 System Purpose
Manages the mechanical trading properties and market calculation hooks for the black-market arbitrator.

## 🌀 Lore & Aesthetic Integration
An old-world survivor with a mechanical glass eye that continuously clicks and shifts focus as it scans local networks. He runs his storefront from a rusted, military-grade cargo container tucked into a blind spot of the Pax Aegis grid. He smells of battery ozone and strong, cheap rolling tobacco.

## ⚙️ Mechanical Specification
*   **Unique Identifier**: `NPC_SILAS_02`.
*   **The High-Frequency Ticker Link**: Unlike standard street vendors who lag behind by 3 hours, Silas holds a `RealTimeTicker = true` status flag. He evaluates the exact heavy-water Deuterium metrics on the exact second the engine executes its hourly tick calculation.
*   **Arbitrage Interaction**: Silas identifies rare high-value electronic salvage, offering a flat $2.0\times$ premium on scrap items if the global market drops into a localized sector recession.

## 🧠 Thought Process & Logic Architecture
Silas serves as a mechanical utility asset. By bypassing the standard 3-tick market delay algorithm applied to normal shopkeepers, his store file reads data parameters directly from the core economic state array, providing high-risk economic play options for attentive players.
