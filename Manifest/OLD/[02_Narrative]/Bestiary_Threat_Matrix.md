# Threat Registry: Standard Units, 3 Gatekeepers, and Primary Anomaly

## 🔍 System Purpose
Documents structural AI archetypes, behavioral routines, and combat phase patterns for combat testing loops.

## 🌀 Lore & Aesthetic Integration
Enemies reflect the cold, unyielding industrialization of the Pax Aegis Consortium, contrasting against the chaotic, fluid frequencies of rogue AI apparitions.

## ⚙️ Mechanical Specification

### Standard Units (Unlockable/Encountered In-Game)
*   **Aegis Spark-Hound**: Fast, low-mass quadrupeds. They sprint along fixed linear paths, aiming to grapple Young Sir and drain his current quick pocket batteries.
*   **Scrap-Drone**: Hovering airborne frames armed with cutting torches. They exploit high wind vectors to hover out of close-range melee bounds.

### The 3 Gatekeeper Bosses (Progression Barriers)
1.  **Gatekeeper 01: The Iron Warden (Physical Wall)**
    *   *Concept*: A heavily armored Pax Aegis enforcement mech guarding Sector 0 Outpost. 
    *   *Mechanic*: Immune to conventional damage vectors. Young Sir must run behind dynamic environment crates to drop its stability metrics, using the Sphere-Cast ground sensor to identify cracks in the concrete floor to blast its grounding nodes.
2.  **Gatekeeper 02: Corrupted Flux-Node (Hybrid Construct)**
    *   *Concept*: A massive, stationary automated power station fully possessed by rogue code.
    *   *Mechanic*: Violently shifts local climate arrays, generating 100% humidity downpours that cause electrical current fields to cascade outward across the room.
3.  **Gatekeeper 03: The Silent Circuit (Digital Assassin)**
    *   *Concept*: A high-velocity, invisible cybernetic specter tracking Young Sir's frequency.
    *   *Mechanic*: Blurs out of sight, forcing the player to track visual distortions in the rain and dust matrices to parry its approaches.

### The Primary Boss: The Digital Phantom (The True Nemesis)
*   *Concept*: The ancient, omnipresent wireless anomaly introduced in Chapter 00. 
*   *Mechanic*: A multi-phase cosmic technology execution. It reconstructs itself using the surrounding physics crates, throwing mass objects across the spatial matrix. It forces Young Sir into an endurance cycle where he must perfectly sync his resonance frequency with the Phantom's shifting wave outputs to survive.

## 🧠 Thought Process & Logic Architecture
Every entity uses a high-performance **Finite State Machine (FSM)**. AI routines evaluate distance, current health matrices, and environment humidity arrays once every few frames rather than running a costly continuous processing cycle.
