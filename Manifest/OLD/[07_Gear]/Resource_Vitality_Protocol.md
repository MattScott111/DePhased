# Resource Vitality Protocol

## Concept
Combat is an energy-negative state. Consumption of combat resources directly depletes the entity's Vitality Pool (`GContext.EnergyPool`). 

## Rules of Engagement
- **Efficiency**: Market-purchased upgrades (Watts) focus on reducing weapon energy consumption.
- **Evolution**: World-found upgrades (Resonance/Core Shifts) unlock new physical capabilities.
- **Sustainability**: Combat capability is throttled by the Energy Pool. Players must choose between upgrading wealth for utility or seeking resonant frequencies to evolve physical capabilities.
