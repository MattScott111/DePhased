## 1. System Overview
Societal factions are not just groups of NPCs; they are Architectural Filters that partition the NMN to control flow and harvest data. 

## 2. Major & Minor Node Hierarchy

### A. The Sovereigns (State & Corp)
* **The Pax Hegemony:** The ruling state. 
    * *Major Hub:* The Aegis Bastion.
    * *NMN Profile:* Sterile, highly structured, high latency.
* **Synapse Core:** The corporate harvester.
    * *Major Hub:* The Processing Grid.
    * *NMN Profile:* Grid-locked, heavily patrolled by Inquisitor Daemons.

### B. The Rogue Elements (Rebels & Tribes)
* **The Static-Breakers (Rebel Clan):**
    * *Major Hub:* The Null-Point Prime.
    * *Minor Nodes:* 5 regional cells.
    * *NMN Profile:* Chaotic, raw, high static but low latency.
* **The Echo-Seekers (Neutral Tribe):**
    * *Major Hub:* The Echo-Spire.
    * *Minor Nodes:* 5 local enclaves (The Hollow Nodes).
    * *NMN Profile:* Hallucinogenic, unstable resonance.
