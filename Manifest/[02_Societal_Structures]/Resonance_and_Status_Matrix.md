# Resonance and Status Matrix

## Meta-Data
- **Archetype:** Logic Processor (Reputation)
- **Classification:** Economy/Society
- **Status:** Draft_01
- **Focus:** Dual-Plane Reputation

---

## 1. System Overview
Reputation in De-Phased operates on two independent tracks. A player must manage both their physical appearance to NPCs and their digital footprint in the NMN.

## 2. The Dual Tracks
* **Plane A (Physical Vibe):** Esteemed vs. Exiled. Determines how physical guards and merchants react to Drift's cylinder.
* **Plane B (Digital Static):** Clean vs. Virus. Determines how NMN daemons and automated security scanners view Drift's data signature.

*Note:* A player can be physically "Esteemed" by walking confidently, while simultaneously being a "Virus" hunted by the digital backend.

## 3. The Exile Mutation
* **Concept:** Extreme negative reputation alters Drift's fundamental code.
* **Mechanic:** If Drift reaches maximum "Exile" status with the Pax Hegemony, his De-Phased digital avatar permanently glows with a harsh red "Tracker Code." This attracts aggressive daemons but passively terrifies lower-level NPCs in Plane A due to his corrupted aura.
